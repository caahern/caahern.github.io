Hand Drawn Typography and T-Shirt Design

As a part of a typography assignment, we were to create 5 experimental hand-drawn typographic designs based upon short texts or quotations. The most successful design was to then be used to generate a T-shirt design which will be produced using the screenprinting facilities at Megalo print studios in Kingston.
