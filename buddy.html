Buddy: A Phone Application for University Students

The challenge in this brief was to help decrease the number of students who drop out of university in their first year. Using statistics from interviews and a number of both low and high quality prototype tests, we were to find the most common issues that many students find hard to cope with and hence suggest ways to address these issues using technology. We were to create a tool that engages students in their new learning environment and create a positive user experience.

The Buddy app is designed as a virtual ‘buddy’ that can help and assist all university students with organisation, socialisation, and relevant resources. It’s the perfect friend because they’re always there and always know how to help. It has the ability to successfully guide students and relieve stress which is a significant achievement in relation to contributing to minimising the increasing rate of university drop outs.
