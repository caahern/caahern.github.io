Personal Logo

First year university assignment, and my first graphic design assignment at university. The brief was to create a two colour logo with the Illustrator pen tool using my own initials. The logo had to incorporate text (Monogram) and image (Symbol) to create a unique identity that visually communicated the characteristics of my own self. The design was required to reflect my own character.
