Too Hip or Not Too Hip

This project was to create a Tumblr website designed around a specific topic and audience of our own choosing. It wasn't about creating a sophisticated or visually stunning web site, but more about demonstrating our knowledge of the unit concepts (audiences, attention, monetization, SEO, online advertising, uses of social networking, etc.) and working within the restrictions of a brief.

My topic was all about dining out in Canberra. More specifically to unique, quirky, and urban restaurants or cafes in the area. I ordered, rated, photographed and discussed the dining experiences.
